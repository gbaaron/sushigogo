Who We Are

Fresh Sushi in Grandville

Sushi GoGo started with a simple idea: everyone deserves access to fresh, delicious sushi without the wait or the fine-dining price tag. Located inside Rivertown Mall in Grandville, Michigan, we bring handcrafted rolls, poke bowls, and Japanese-inspired favorites right to the food court.

Every roll is made to order using quality fish, crisp vegetables, and house-made sauces. We believe that fast food doesn't have to mean compromising on freshness or flavor. From our signature Dragon Roll to a simple California Roll, each piece is rolled with care.

Whether you're grabbing a quick lunch between stores, picking up dinner for the family, or treating yourself to a poke bowl, Sushi GoGo is here to make your day a little more delicious.
